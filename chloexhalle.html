Chloe Bailey

Born: July 1, 1998

Halle Bailey

Born: March 27, 2000

Info

Sister duo Chloe and Halle are singers who can produce beats (Chloe) and play instruments (Halle). They signed to Beyonce in 2013 after one of their many YouTube covers went viral. The sister are also both talented actors, appearing in works such as Disney's "Let it Shine," and Tyler Perry's "Meet the Browns" for example. Both also play fan loved charater's in ABC Family's "Grown-ish," which Halle Bailey recently left to film the live action version of Disney's Ariel. They have two albums, "The Kids Are Alright" and "Ungodly Hour."
