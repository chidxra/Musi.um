Jermaine Lamarr Cole

Born: January 28, 1985

Info

Born on January 28, 1985 in a U.S. Army base in Frankfurt, Jermaine Lamarr Cole, or also know as J. Cole, has lived to become a American hip hop recording artist and record producer. His hit album, 2014 Forest Hills Drive, came out in December 2014 and went #1 on the Billboard 200. It won many awards,including Billboard Rap Album of the Year, and later became certified double platinum.
