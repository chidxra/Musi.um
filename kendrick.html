Kendrick Lamar

Info

Kendrick Lamar Duckworth is an American rapper/singer/songwriter who was born June 17, 1987 is Comptoon, California. Lamar had recieved not one but two Grammy's for two of his most popular albums, DAMN., and To Pimp A ButterFly, both in the category of Best Rap Album. Today, Kendricks most loved album is DAMN., with "DNA.", "LOYALTY.(FEAT.RIHANNA)", "HUMBLE.", and "LOVE." being his most loved songs on that album.
