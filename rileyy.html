Rileyy Lanez

Born: February 25, 2001

Info

Rileyy Lanez is an artist that got signed due to the popularity of her song covers on instagram. She is known for a strong voice and her moving lyrics, writing about her experiences with love, and life in general, as she dedicated her song "When I See You (Lamont's Song)" to her late father, who died in a Bronx shooting in 2014
