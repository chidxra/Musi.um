Came Together: September 27, 2016

Info

The 'Why Don't We Boys' are a boy band of five, originated in Los Angeles. They consist of: Jonah Marais, Corbyn Besson, Daniel Seavey, Jack Avery and Zach Herron. Their band was formed September 27th 2016, then announced September 28th 2016. The band has released 8 singles, and a song as a featured artist. That song was Help Me Help You by Logan Paul, which was part of their success, popularity and was their best song yet. A pop dream for the 21st century, vocal harmony group Why Don't We comprise social media stars from across America.
