Willow Camille Reign Smith

Born: October 31, 2000

Info

Willow Camille Reign Smith, known as Willow Smith, was born on October 31, 2000 in Los Angelos to actor Will Smith and punk singer Jada Smith, second to Jaden Smith, third of all of Will Smith's children. While popular for her childhood song, "Whip My Hair," she decided to take her music to the punk scene, becoming an alt icon for black music lovers, and POC in general.
